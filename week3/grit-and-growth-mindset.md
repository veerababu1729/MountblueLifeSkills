# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit Video
**Question:** Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines. Use your own words.  
**Answer:** Angela says grit means you love a goal for a long time and keep trying hard. It beats smartness for winning in tough places like school or contests.

## 2. Growth Mindset Intro
**Question:** Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines in your own words.  
**Answer:** Fixed mindset says you are born smart or not, so you skip hard things. Growth mindset says you grow skills with work, so you try hard and learn from fails.

## 3. Internal Locus
**Question:** What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?  
**Answer:** Internal locus means you think your work and choices make your results, not luck or others. Key point: it keeps you going, like kids praised for effort who pick hard puzzles.

## 4. Build Growth Mindset
**Question:** What are the key points mentioned by speaker to build growth mindset (explanation not needed).  
**Answer:**  
- Think you can solve things.  
- Ask if your ideas are true.  
- Make your own learning plan.  
- Like the hard work.

## 5. My Ideas for Action
**Question:** What are your ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset?  
**Answer:** Preserverance, quality in execution and quantity in hardwork.
